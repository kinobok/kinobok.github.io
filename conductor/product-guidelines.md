# Brand Aesthetic
- **Minimalist Dark & Cinematic:** The UI defaults to a sleek, minimalist dark mode that reduces eye strain and allows vibrant movie posters to pop. Accents should feel "cinematic" (e.g., deep, rich colors) without cluttering the interface.

# User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Map-First Navigation:** The interactive Leaflet map is the primary exploration tool. Markers and popups must be highly responsive and deeply integrated with the showtime data.
- **Fast Filtering:** Client-side matching must provide zero-latency updates. Toggling filters or uploading a watchlist should immediately reflect on both the map and the sidebar.
- **Mobile-Centric Drawer:** On mobile devices, the map should dominate the screen real estate. The movie list and filters should be housed in an intuitive, swipe-up bottom drawer to maximize spatial exploration.

# Prose & Copywriting
- **Minimalist Tone:** Text should be kept to an absolute minimum. Prioritize universally understood icons (e.g., Lucide React) and clear data visualizations (numbers, badges) over verbose explanations. Keep UI labels direct and concise.
