# Initial Concept
kinꚘbok is a privacy-first web application that matches a user's Letterboxd watchlist with Warsaw cinema showtimes locally in the browser.

# Target Audience
- **Warsaw Cinephiles:** Frequent cinema-goers who actively track their movies on Letterboxd.
- **Tourists and Expats:** International visitors and local residents looking for screenings in their preferred language.
- **Indie Film Fans:** Audiences specifically seeking out independent, art-house, and rare film screenings.

# Core Value Propositions
- **Privacy-First:** All data matching is processed client-side, ensuring users' watchlists remain private.
- **Client-side Speed:** Instantaneous filtering and matching powered by browser-level data processing.
- **Broad Coverage:** Comprehensive inclusion of major theater chains alongside smaller venues.
- **Championing Indie Cinema:** A strong focus on surfacing rare screenings and independent cinemas to promote lesser-known films.

# Future Expansion
- **Geographic Expansion:** Scaling the Python scraper to support additional Polish cities beyond Warsaw.
- **Enhanced Features:** Introducing new functionality such as direct ticket booking links, advanced filters, and more.

# Hosting and Sustainability
- **Open-source / Free:** Built as a free, open-source tool for the community, with a planned hosting strategy via GitHub Pages.
